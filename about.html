Lulu's Beauty and Bar is a nail, lash and massage therapy enterprise
which offers sophisticated services for both women and men to look pretty
and feel their way for any occasion, with their body relaxed.

The business will offer all round beauty and massage therapy experience consisting
of Pedicure, Manicure, Gel, lash installation, body, back and neck massage. The company also offers beauty services for events such as parties and
weddings.

  • Mission

    • To inspire confidence and enhance our client’s natural beauty through our
    expert services, exceptional customer care , and commitment to excellence.
    It’s our goal to promote a culture of self-care and wellness, where every
    client can relax, recharge and rejuvenate their mind , body and soul.

  • Vision

    • Our business is committed to making our clients feel beautiful inside and out,
    by providing top-quality beauty and spa services that enhance their Natural
    beauty. We want to empower our clients to express their unique style,
    personality and beauty through our expert services and personalised
    approach.
